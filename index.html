How to Play

  • Click "Play" to start the game.
  • Select the difficulty of the level you want to play.
    1. Easy
      There are 16 tiles on the board. Try to find the 8 different pairs as fast as possible.
    2. Intermediate
      There are 24 cards on the board. Try to find the 12 different pairs as fast as possible.
    3. Hard
      There are 32 cards on the board. Try to find the 16 different pairs as fast as possible.
  • Once your game starts, you will then select two tiles on the board.
    - If they match, they will stay face up on the board.
    - If they don't match, they will flip back over - try and remember where all the cards are if you don't get a matching pair.
  • If at any point you want to restart your game, you can click on the restart arrow in the top right corner of the game board.
  • Have fun and try to beat your previous time and flip count!
